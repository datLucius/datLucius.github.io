Lucius Henry Weeks, Jr.
My grandfather use to give me a pocket knife everytime he would visit. Overtime, I realized he was giving me his personality expressed via pocket knives. For his 80th birthday, I devoted 80 hours into painting his portrait alongside our knife collection.
