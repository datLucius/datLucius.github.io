Lucius Henry Weeks, Jr.
My grandfather use to give me a pocket knife everytime he would visit. Overtime, I realized he was giving me his personality expressed via pocket knives. For his 80th birthday, I devoted 80 hours into painting his portrait alongside our knife collection.
awarded Best in Show @ the Halsey's Young Contemporary Show 2013
Hex Mix
This is a group project I worked on at the Iron Yard. During this project, I was required to step up to the plate in Angular JS and build functionality that let's users create / search color palettes. My front end team worked with a a back end team to discuss concept, data structure, and learn how to collaborate using github.
Tech used: Angular JS, Jquery, Javascript, Spectrum, HMTL, CSS, git
